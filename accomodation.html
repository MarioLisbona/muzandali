ULUWATU SURF VILLAS

We've reserved accommodation for most guests to stay at Ulutwatu Surf Villas. USV sits cliff-top, overlooking Temples break and has direct private access to the surf.

www.uluwatusurfvillas.com/

There are 16 villas with 2 - 5 rooms each. This means most people will be sharing the villa common areas with friends or family. It will cost about USD $150/night per person. Breakfast at Mana is included. If you are staying at USV:

Check-In: Wednesday, March 29, 2023

Check-Out: Monday, April 3, 2023

Once you RSVP, we'll contact you on WhatsApp or Messenger to help organise a room.

AROUND USV

Yoga: USV offers 7:30am daily yoga classes in their Morning Light studio about USD $7 .

Surfing: You can access Temples directly from the USV staircase down the cliffside. USV also has complimentary surfboards available for you to use, if you'd like to give surfing a try. Temples isn't really a beginner wave, but there are beginner spots nearby at Padang Padang and Dreamland Beach.

Mana Restaurant:Mana is part of USV and serves breakfast (free for USV guests), lunch, dinner and drinks. You can view their menu here.
